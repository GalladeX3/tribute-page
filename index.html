Tribute To My Favorite Videogame Characters

Celebrating two brothers who defined platforming heroes.

Luigi — The Unsung Hero

Brave. Loyal. Endlessly underrated.

Luigi Mario may live in his brother’s shadow, but he’s a hero in his own right: cool under pressure, kinder than most, and fearless when it matters. From haunted mansions to kart circuits, Luigi shows up — and gets it done.

Whether he’s vacuuming ghosts, nailing the perfect jump, or saving friends with that signature nervous smile, Luigi proves that true heroes don’t need the spotlight.

Mario — The Steadfast Icon

Reliable. Adventurous. Timeless.

Mario is the series’ steady center — optimistic, adaptable, and always the first to jump into danger. His silhouette is universal, his moveset precise, and his spirit relentlessly upbeat.

From tight platformers to sports and karting, Mario evolves without losing the simple, readable design and responsive feel that set the gold standard for game controls.
